A Norwegian Cloud Infrastructure for Research and Education

Fast, standardized servers and storage for the Norwegian higher education sector. Get access to a self-service, modern and secure IT infrastructure, available when you need it.

The Future of IT is in the Clouds

In the past, getting access to IT resources has been a long-winded process. The UH IaaS service delivers standardized servers and storage quickly with less effort. Our self-service platform is built on OpenStack, an industry standard for cloud computing.

Self-Service Infrastructure

Compute

Block Storage

Our fully self-service block storage allows you to create and expand volumes on the fly.

Object Storage

If you need to collect, store and analyze data on a larger scale, we offer robust and highly scalable object storage based on Ceph Coming soon.

Geo Redundancy

We have data centres located in Bergen and Oslo for high availability and redundancy.
